# Nutrisipe 🍳

Nutrisipe is a modern, social recipe-sharing platform designed for home cooks. It transforms the often overwhelming experience of finding daily meal inspiration into a warm, engaging, and highly visual scroll. Instead of sifting through text-heavy food blogs or corporate SaaS dashboards, users are immersed in a "food-first" feed tailored for discovery, remixing, and community.

## What is it for?

The core job of Nutrisipe is to answer the daily question: **"What should I cook tonight?"**
It gives everyday cooks a relaxed, playful space to find recipe ideas, save them for later, and recreate them with their own spin. The tone is encouraging—food is treated as a pleasure rather than strict optimization.

## Key Features & Selling Points

### 1. 🍴 Recipe Forking (Remixing dishes like code)
Nutrisipe introduces a unique "forking" mechanism to cooking. Users can take an existing recipe, swap out ingredients (e.g., make it dairy-free or add an extra spice), and save it as their own version. The platform traces lineages, so the original creator always receives credit as their recipe inspires new global variations.

### 2. 📊 Nutrition Decoded
No manual calculations required. Every recipe automatically aggregates and displays nutritional macros (calories, protein, fats, carbs) per serving based on an underlying, meticulously managed ingredient database.

### 3. 📱 A Feed that Feeds You
The platform emphasizes a lively social experience without the toxic noise. Users can follow their favorite cooks, save dishes into categorized collections, and engage via comments and real-time messaging, all while the recipe stays at the center stage instead of vanity metrics.

### 4. 🗓 Meal Planning & Grocery Lists
Users can drag and drop their saved recipes into a weekly meal planner. The app can then automatically compile the required items into a streamlined grocery shopping list, categorized for efficiency.

### 5. 🛡 Admin Command Center
Nutrisipe ships with a robust administrative suite to manage the ecosystem:
- **Dashboard & Analytics:** Real-time insights into platform usage and engagement.
- **Citizen Management:** Tools to manage the user base.
- **Content Moderation:** Dedicated spaces for handling reports and keeping the community safe.
- **Ingredient Database Editor:** A powerful tool for admins to add, tweak, and maintain the global ingredient records that power the macro calculations.

## Design Philosophy

The application strictly adheres to a set of premium design principles:
- **Food First:** High contrast, visually striking interfaces that ensure the food photography is the hero.
- **Delight in the Scroll:** Dynamic layouts, subtle 3D background elements, and smooth micro-animations make the app feel alive and responsive.
- **Approachable Complexity:** Powerful tools (like macro breakdowns and meal planners) are always available but elegantly tucked away so they don't intrude on a user's casual browsing experience.
